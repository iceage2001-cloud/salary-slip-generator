🏛️ Tax Regime Selection (FY 2025-26)

New Tax Regime (Default from FY 2024-25): Lower slab rates, no exemptions/deductions (no 80C, HRA, LTA). Standard Deduction ₹75,000. Slabs: Up to ₹3L = Nil | ₹3L–7L = 5% | ₹7L–10L = 10% | ₹10L–12L = 15% | ₹12L–15L = 20% | Above ₹15L = 30%.
Tax Rebate u/s 87A: Full rebate if net taxable income ≤ ₹7 Lakhs (tax = ₹0).
Old Tax Regime: Higher rates but allows exemptions: HRA, LTA, 80C (₹1.5L), 80D, Standard Deduction ₹50,000, etc. Slabs: Up to ₹2.5L = Nil | ₹2.5L–5L = 5% | ₹5L–10L = 20% | Above ₹10L = 30%.
Tax Rebate u/s 87A: Full rebate if net taxable income ≤ ₹5 Lakhs (tax = ₹0).

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free? Yes, completely free. No signup or login required.

Is my data saved anywhere? No. All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

What is HRA? House Rent Allowance. Typically 40% of Basic (non-metro) or 50% (metro cities) as per Indian income tax rules.

What is the PF deduction rate? Employee PF contribution is 12% of Basic Salary as per EPF Act, India.

What is ESI? Employee State Insurance. Employee contributes 0.75% of gross salary. Applicable only if gross ≤ ₹21,000/month.

What is Professional Tax? A state-level tax in India (varies by state). Maximum ₹200/month. Not applicable in all states.

Can I use this for court/legal purposes? This tool generates a standard format slip for reference. For legally binding documents, consult your HR or CA.
